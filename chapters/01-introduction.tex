\chapter{Introduction}

Creativity is of prime importance in Carnatic music. Carnatic music is
traditionally live music. Even today there is little production/editing. It began
much before amplifiers were invented let alone recording equipment or multi-track
recording. It has been evolving for centuries now. To enable live music without
restricting the musician would require some rules that the musicians can agree upon.
These rules form the basis of Carnatic Rhythms.\footnote{The word 'rule' is
misleading. 'Identity' is more apt. It is more like the rule that three strikes is
an out in baseball.} These rules make live music so easy --- only live music so
easy. When you learn these rules, you will discover that they are indispensable.
Another advantage of this framework is that musicians who have never met before can
sit down and create an amazing live music. What western musicians would call a
jam-session is essentially what happens in a carnatic concert. However, the rules we
talk about give so much structure to the music that the outcome is simply
mind-blowing.\footnote{This sentence is totally untrue if the musicians have not done
their homework.} This is true for both rhythmic and melodic improvisation in the
context of carnatic music. The following chapters explain some of the concepts of
rhythm in carnatic music.
